Alexei Anatolievich Navalny (Russian: Алексей Анатольевич Навальный, born 4 June 1976) 
is a Russian opposition leader, lawyer, and anti-corruption activist. 
He came to international prominence by organizing anti-government demonstrations 
and running for office to advocate reforms against corruption in Russia, 
and against President Vladimir Putin and his government. 
Putin avoids directly referring to Navalny by name. 
Navalny was a Russian Opposition Coordination Council member. 
He is the leader of the Russia of the Future party
and the founder of the Anti-Corruption Foundation (FBK).
